OUR STORY

Tamashi was founded by a close group of college friends who fell in love with Tokyo and ramen. Having tasted the one of the best, we went on a quest to find the best ramen in LA â€” and were unfortunately very disappointed.

This sparked our (neon) lightbulb to bring Tokyo a new "home" to LA. We recreated the usual Japanese ordering vending machine as iPads and hired multiple chefs from Japan to break down the chemistry-laden recipes and have them teadiously house-made from scratch with natural ingredients (and cold sweat and tears!). We shocked the ramen traditionalists by also offering gluten-free option, 100% chicken and vegan broth. "Tamashi" means soul and opened its door to the public in 2014. It has since become a must visit frequented by A-listers, hipsters and blacklisters.
